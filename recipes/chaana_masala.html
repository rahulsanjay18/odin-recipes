Chaana Masala

Mother's Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dry chickpeas
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 2 Green chilies or 1 tbsp red chili powder (dependent on intensity of powder)
  • 3/2 teaspoon cumin coriander powder mix
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1-3/2 inch of ginger
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 tsp ghee

Directions

  1. Wash the beans
  2. Soak the beans in a lot of water (3 to 4x water per beans) overnight or for at least 5 hours
  3. Remove all the water, rinse the beans
  4. Put it in a metal container
  5. Put that in a pressure cooker for 4-5 whistles on the highest setting
  6. While that is going prepare the gravy
  7. Cut the onions and tomatoes in big chunks
  8. Put 2 tsp oil and 2 tsp ghee in a deep pan
  9. Let the ghee melt
  10. Put 1/4 tsp sugar
  11. Let the sugar melt and brown
  12. When sugar browns, put the chopped onion in.Cook for a minute
  13. When onion becomes translucent put the ginger and garlic
  14. Make sure the garlic does not burn
  15. Then put the chili in it
  16. Put the cumin/coriander mix in and the garam masala and turmeric powder
  17. Cook for about a minute
  18. Then put tomatoes in it
  19. Cover it up to cook the tomatoes
  20. When tomatoes get cooked and get mushy, open the lid and keep it open and stir until you see oil coming out/li>
  21. When oil comes out shut the gas and let it cool down
  22. Grind everything (in the gravy) up
  23. Put it back in the pan
  24. Cook the gravy for half a minute
  25. Put the chickpeas (that were in the pressure cooker) in the pan
  26. Cook it while covered
  27. After 5 min taste it
  28. If everything is okay, take kasoori methi and 1 to 3/2 lemon juice crush it in you hand and put it in the pan
  29. Cook it while covered for half a minute
