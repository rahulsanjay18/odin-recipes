Pepper Saak

Mother's Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp of oil
  • 2 1/2 cup of cut Pepper
  • 1/4 and 1 spoon of besan
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander cumin powder
  • Pinch of hing

Directions

  1. Roast besan in the microwave for 30 seconds and then put all ingredients in besan.
  2. Put oil in pan, let it warm and than put peppers in it and cook it until Peppers soft. Than put besan in it and mix it.
  3. Cook it for 5 minutes.
